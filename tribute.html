Nikola Tesla

Time Line

  • 1882 - conceives of AC induction motor
  • 1882 - Begins working for Edison
  • 1883 - Builds polyphase AC motor
  • 1886 - Builds Tesla Eletric Light & Manufacturing
  • 1887 - Builds first induction motor
  • 1888 - Begins work at Westinghouse
  • 1891 - Invents Tesla coil
  • 1892 - Discovers x-rays
  • 1893 - Demonstrates radio
  • 1895 - Harnesses Niagra Falls
  • 1898 - Develops remote control
  • 1898 - Develops electric igniter for gasoline engines
  • 1900 - Begins wardenclyffe transmitter
  • 1906 - Develops bladeless turbine
  • 1906 - Wardenclyffe project abandon
  • 1917 - Receives the Edison medal
  • 1928 - Patented the helicopter
  • 1943 - Dies penniless
  • 1944 - Awarded a patent for the Radio
